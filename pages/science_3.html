OpenCL

A brief introduction for NVIDIA CUDA programmers

Talk in the module Parallel Computing as part of the study course Applied Physics at the RheinAhrCampus (University of Applied Sciences Koblenz).

Contributer: Jan-Maximilian Beneke
Instructor: Prof. Dr. Schmidt and Prof. Dr. Jaekel
Keywords: Parallel Computing, OpenCL, NVIDIA CUDA, ATI Stream, AMD, INTEL, Khronos Group, Matrix Multiplication
Date: Date: 16th January 2012

Abstract

This project gives an insight to OpenCL especially for NVIDIA CUDA programmers. It describes the basic elements and functions as well as the design goals of OpenCL and demonstrates them in a matrix multiplication application. A benchmark underlines the capabilities of OpenCL.
